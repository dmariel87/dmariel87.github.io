Charlotte, North Carolina

Top 5 Fun Things To Do In Charlotte

  1. Discovery Place - Discovery Place is a great way to speand an afternoon with family. It's very kid friendly with multiple exibits for different ages but its also a great place for adults who love museums and learning.
  2. U.S National Whitewater Center - Over 30 different activities for everyone from the outdoor enthusiast to the casual hiker.Great for families and even pets!
  3. Freedom Park - Freedom Park is almost 100 acres and is a nice escape from the hustle and bustle of the city. Come to enjoy the playground with the kids or catch a workout class, take a walk or have a picnic.
  4. BB&T Ballpark - Home field for the Harlem Knights come enjoy a baseball game or one of the many events at the field.
  5. Charlotte Motor Speedway - If you have a taste for the fast life make sure you visit the speedway and either take a tour or take a ride in an actual race car!

Optimist Hall!

This wonderful food hall has been open for less than a year but has gained such popularity due to the restaurants it houses. Get your dumpling fix with The Dumpling Lady and gelato with Honeysuckle Gelato. There are new vendors opening very soon as well!
