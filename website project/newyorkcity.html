New York City

Top 5 Fun Things To Do In New York City!

  1. Times Square- From great shopping to great hotels Times Square is home to the brightest lights in the world!
  2. Empire State Building- Go to the top of this historic building to see great views of the city.
  3. Statue of Liberty- Beautiful monument that represents the heart of America.
  4. Brooklyn Bridge Park- Great for outdoor activities and views of the Brooklyn Bridge.
  5. Rockefeller Center - Enjoy the Top of the Rock exhibit, shopping and ice skating in the winter.
