Las Vegas

Top 5 Fun Things To Do In Las Vegas!

  1. Las Vegas Strip- From great shopping to great hotels Las Vegas Strip is home to the best casinos and hotels in the world!
  2. Red Rock Canyon- Beautiful hiking trails and wonderful views. Bring water!
  3. MGM Grand- Perhaps the most famous casino on the strip.
  4. Freemont Street Experience- Open air mall with great entertainment.
  5. The Adventuredome Indoor Theme Park - Indoor amusement park located right on the Vegas Strip.
